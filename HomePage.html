The Belco Alliance's governance reflects its collaborative nature. Built on strong institutional relationships, mutual trust and the common strategic goals, the Alliance's governance is done in different and changing teams depending the activity and the participating partners.

Our alliance

The BELCO Founders

Initially founded by Fontys UAS (the Netherlands), UCL University College (Denmark), CBS International Business School (Germany), BBA INSEEC (France) and LAB University of Applied Sciences (Finland) as a result of a fruitful and strength-combined collaboration, the BELCO Alliance has been growing into an interactive network between European and Non-European Higher Education Institutions with a strong international focus.

Our organization

BELCO Alliance
The Belco Alliance has a transparent and participative organizational structure.

Alliance at a glance
Grew out of the commitment to sustainable and high-quality seeking strategic partnership, the consortium is a reply to an increasing demand for a new way of international collaboration between higher education institutions fostering their educational programs in order to prepare proactive professionals with strong inter-cultural and economic awareness and able to efficiently work and contribute to our global society.

Alliance organization
The Belco alliance is constructed as an organizational structure with horizontal communication. Our president, Dr. Maurice Cuypers, values accessible communication between all partners and thereby we aim at creating synergy. Ensuring transparency and participation. Our shared goal is to become a contributing globally with strong collaboration between our partners.

Alliance Partners
The BELCO Alliance continuously consolidates the higher education community and its relationship with international partners by promoting cooperation among its members and affiliates and providing a “space” in which member representatives can meet, interact and develop their collaborative projects.
