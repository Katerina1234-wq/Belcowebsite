Around the world with Belco

Around the World with Belco celebrates the people, programs, and partnerships that make our alliance truly global. We believe that education, innovation, and social impact thrive when shaped by diverse perspectives and cross-cultural collaboration.

Positive aspects:

Global Citizenship

  • Understand global challenges like sustainability, inequality, education, and peacebuilding
  • Contribute to meaningful, cross-border solutions
  • Engage in volunteering or community impact projects
  • Become an advocate for inclusion, diversity, and global cooperation

Career-Boosting Experience

  • Gain international work or internship experience (often part of exchange options)
  • Build a global network of mentors, colleagues, and friends
  • Develop leadership, adaptability, and problem-solving skills
  • Boost your CV/resume with unique international credentials
